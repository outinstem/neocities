About UoSTEM@BU

We are the Undergraduate arm of the oSTEM chapter at Boston University. Here, we aim to propagate oSTEM's national mission by empowering LGBTQ+ undergraduate students who are studying or planning to study STEM to succeed personally, academically, and professionally by cultivating an environment and community that nurtures innovation, leadership, and advocacy. Any part-time or full-time student of Boston University who is academically involved in a STEM field is welcome to join our community!

UoSTEM@BU was founded in Fall 2018 in response to a need for community and professional development among the LGBTQ+ community of STEM students at BU. We believe in empowering students today so that they can empower the future of the LGBTQ+ community after they graduate.

If you are interested in joining our community, please contact us or come to one of our meetings! We hope to see you there!

Our Mission

"UoSTEM aims to propagate the mission of the national organization by empowering LGBTQ+ people in STEM to succeed personally, academically, and professionally by cultivating an environment and community that nurtures innovation, leadership, and advocacy."
