Congratulations Dana Almberg, our new Administrative Secretary!

Thursday, June 13, 2019 at 2:17 PM

We had a lot of interest in the open eBoard position, and after a lot of deliberation have selected Dana as the Administrative Secretary for the 2019-2020 academic year. Dana is a Junior majoring in Cell and Molecular Biology and Genetics and minoring in Public Health. She loves global health research, aerial dancing and wants you to know she's really excited for oSTEM this year!

Additionally, we're about halfway through Pride month now! Check out the official Boston Pride website for upcoming events. If you want to get this news and more directly in your inbox, subscribe to our mailing list either here or at mailchimp!

Of course, this means that we are saying goodbye to Catherine Della Santina, who formerly held Dana's position. Catherine is moving on to bigger things, but hopes to remain just as involved with oSTEM as before!
